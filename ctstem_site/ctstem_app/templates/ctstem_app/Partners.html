Partners

Our team brings together unparalleled disciplinary expertise, existing K-12 school connections, and CT and STEM education experience. With their extensive work in computer simulation and technology innovation, as well as in the role of cyberinfrastructure in computational modeling, and in effective STEM education and curriculum development, they form a uniquely complementary team for undertaking the proposed CE21 program focused on embedded-CT.

Graduate Students

Each year, 10 Northwestern graduate students with a computational focus to their research are paired with 10 local secondary school teachers through the NSF-funded Reach for the Stars GK12 program. These graduate student - teacher pairs develop and pilot test the raw materials used in this project.
